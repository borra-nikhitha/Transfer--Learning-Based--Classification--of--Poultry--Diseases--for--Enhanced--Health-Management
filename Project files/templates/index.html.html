Disease Classification

Our system can accurately identify and classify four major poultry health conditions

🦠 Salmonella

Bacterial infection causing severe digestive issues and potentially fatal septicemia in poultry.

  • Diarrhea and digestive distress
  • Lethargy and reduced activity
  • Decreased appetite
  • Dehydration symptoms
  • Fever and general illness

🦅 Newcastle Disease

Highly contagious viral disease affecting the respiratory and nervous systems of birds.

  • Respiratory distress
  • Nervous system symptoms
  • Reduced egg production
  • Diarrhea and digestive issues
  • High mortality rates

🪱 Coccidiosis

Parasitic disease affecting the intestinal tract, particularly dangerous for young birds.

  • Bloody diarrhea
  • Significant weight loss
  • Stunted growth
  • Reduced feed conversion
  • Increased mortality

✅ Healthy

No disease detected - bird shows normal, healthy behavior and physiological indicators.

  • Active and alert behavior
  • Good appetite and feeding
  • Normal egg production
  • Healthy appearance
  • Normal growth rates
